══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    PROMOTION GATE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Lifecycle:  DRAFT → PROPOSE → VOTE → COMMIT

   DRAFT:     Private, only creator sees
   PROPOSE:   Published to governance
   VOTE:      Weighted median (§72)
   COMMIT:    Projection persists, auto-recomputes

   Committed projections are still light blue.
   They never become truth. They become endorsed analysis.
