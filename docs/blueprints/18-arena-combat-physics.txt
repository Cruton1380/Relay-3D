ARENA COMBAT PHYSICS — MOVEMENT IS POWER
=========================================
Master Plan refs: §39, §41.5, §43, §43.10, §68


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    KINETIC INPUT RATE  (§43.10.14)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   KIR(t) = Σ (velocity(zone) × mass(zone) × continuity(zone))

   zone = body segment (ROOT / MID / UPPER / EDGE)
   velocity = camera-tracked motion speed
   mass = zone weight coefficient
   continuity = multiplier for unbroken motion (resets on pause)

   Stillness       = 0 AP generated
   Continuous spin  = AP accumulates over time
   Break in motion  = continuity resets to 1.0

   Nunchucks: highest sustained KIR (continuous rotation)
   Guard stance: zero KIR (no motion, no AP)


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    DUAL-CHANNEL SIGNAL
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   CHANNEL 1: COMMAND (what spell)
      ├── gesture recognition (body zones)
      ├── light flash (blade reflection grammar, §43.10)
      └── tap percussion (blade tap patterns)

   CHANNEL 2: MAGNITUDE (how powerful)
      ├── fire light intensity
      ├── motion energy (KIR)
      └── sustained commitment time

   Combined → Spell Output:
      Command selects the spell
      Magnitude scales the effect
      Higher KIR = more Power available


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    CPI — COMMAND PRECISION INDEX
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   CPI = f(signal clarity,
           semantic density,
           throughput,
           resource efficiency)

   Higher CPI → lower Power cost per spell
   Device-agnostic: keyboard, voice, blade, gesture all measured

   A precise gesture costs less Power than a sloppy one.
   Precision is rewarded. Flailing is penalized.

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    TREE HEALTH POOL
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Combat HP = TreeMass × Confidence × Integrity

   Damage reduces temporary combat HP.
   Real tree is NEVER permanently harmed by arena combat.
   Arena is a sandbox (§68) — bounded volatility.

   A thicker, healthier tree has more combat HP.
   Building your real tree = building your combat strength.


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    CROWD AGGREGATE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Spectators → Presence markers → Sap flow to arena

   Crowd engagement amplifies:
      ├── charging attacks (visible, interruptible)
      ├── terrain voting (crowd shapes the arena)
      └── Shine (§109) on combatants

   Crowd influence is toggleable per arena rules (§71.5).
   Crowd never overrides combat mechanics directly.


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    STACK RESOLUTION  (MTG-style LIFO)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Action 1 (cast spell)       → onto stack
   Action 2 (counter-spell)    → onto stack
   Action 3 (counter-counter)  → onto stack

   Resolution: top-down (LIFO)
   Action 3 resolves first
   Then Action 2
   Then Action 1 (if not countered)

   Deterministic. Replayable. Every action is a filament.
