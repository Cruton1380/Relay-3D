══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                 CONSERVATION LAWS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   MASS:
     Input mass = Output mass + Waste + Emissions
     Enforced at every processing stage

   FINANCIAL:
     DEBIT = CREDIT at every TransferPacket
     NET = 0 across the chain

   EVIDENCE:
     Every transformation requires attestation
     Missing evidence = fog on that segment
     Fog accumulates = wilt on parent branch

   Conservation is not a rule. Conservation is physics.
   Violation is structurally impossible without a scar.
