══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    CAMERA DETECTION LAYER
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Camera opt-in (§39.7) with explicit detection levels:

   VIDEO_ONLY         → video feed, no tracking
   TRAINING           → SCV learns your movements
   GESTURE_DETECTION  → body-zone tracking active
   FULL_DETECTION     → projectile + impact + terrain

   Full detection tracks:
     ├── projectile trajectory (paintball, arrow, dart)
     ├── body-zone impact location (head/torso/limb)
     ├── paint color → element mapping
     │     red = fire, blue = ice, green = earth,
     │     yellow = lightning
     ├── velocity + angle of impact
     ├── player movement and posture (KIR)
     └── territory coverage (paint on surfaces)


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    AR OVERLAY  (~40% opacity)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Physical layer: 100% opacity (reality always primary)
   AR layer: semi-transparent overlay on top

   Paint impact on wall →
      digital fracture mark, scorching, structural crack

   Paint impact on player →
      armor crack, energy dissipation, shield effect
      scaled to severity (SCV-tracked precision)

   Terrain →
      territory heatmap (team paint = team color glow)
      destruction accumulates over the match

   AR never obscures physical safety information.
   AR renders on personal devices, not projected into space.


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    METRICS TRACKED (per player)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   ├── Precision %  (shots on target / shots fired)
   ├── Headshot ratio  (head zone hits)
   ├── KIR during match  (movement energy)
   ├── Territory control %  (paint coverage area)
   ├── Team coordination score  (proximity + timing)
   ├── Cover transitions  (how often using terrain)
   ├── Flanking rate  (positional advantage frequency)
   └── Tactical positioning  (heat map of movement)


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    DAMAGE MODEL
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

   Physical impact detected →
   Power calculation (velocity × zone × element) →
   Shield absorption (if any active) →
   Health reduction →
   Respawn timer / elimination

   Paintball markers = spell weapons
   Paint colors = elements
   Firing = spell casting with Power cost
   Physical projectile IS the digital spell
